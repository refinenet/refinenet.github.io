Bibliographical Notes

The ideas in this chapter were first presented in Liberating Data Refinement [22]. The consequences of allowing extra operations in classes for behavioural subtyping are also studied by Liskov and Wing [148]. Their ``constraint rule" is closely related to our alphabet extension rule. This issue is discussed in an Object-Z like context by Helke and Santen [113].
